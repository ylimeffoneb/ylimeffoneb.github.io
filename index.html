I made a website! Here is a cool video of Alan Lomax talking about Sacred Harp! Alan Lomax collected a historic archive of American folk songs.

Lomax is accredited with bringing the music of singers such as Lead Belly, Pete Seeger, and Woodie Guthrie to a popular audience. He traveled around the United States documenting regional folk music traditions; in the process, he produced thousands of significant feild recordings that were subsequently preserved in the Library of Congress's Archive of Folk Song, where he served as the Archivist. Here is a video of one of my favorite Lomax recordings with Appalachian folk singer Roscoe Holcomb. Before moving to LA, I lived in Maryland and went for many hikes and drives through Appalachia. I listened to Lomax's Appalachian recordings a lot during that time.

I'd love to travel around and make my own field recordings one day!
